Dr Robotnic (Discord Bot)

Active in 185 Discord servers, serving more than 40,000 users, It provides functionality to dynamically create voice channels for users as they are needed. This declutters the chatroom and gives user's control over their personal voice channel.

  • Languages: Python, SQL
  • Libraries: discord.py
  • Database: SQLite
  • Hosting: My Home Server
