How I Make Things

All of my projects are made completely from scratch. I don't use templates, tutorials, or large frameworks. Almost all of my knowledge is self-taught through trial and error and I've spent hours learning new languages, frameworks, and tools. My biggest resources are official documentation, online examples like stake overflow, and the results of my mistakes.

Project Quality

I do not like to create janky results. I want other coders to look at my code and be able to see that I've put in the time to learn the best way to do something for my use case. I want to share my knowledge in a way that is easy to understand and build upon.
