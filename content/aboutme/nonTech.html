Life Outside Computers

Though I spend a lot of time immersed in code, I try to keep life well-balanced. I enjoy learning Spanish, spending time outdoors hiking or cycling, and playing local multiplayer games with friends, especially chaotic couch party games where everyone’s laughing in the same room. I’ve also played volleyball, tennis, and hockey competitively during school. Travel has played a big part in shaping my outlook too. I’ve lived on two continents, explored southern Africa, and been part of a cultural exchange to Spain. These experiences help me stay open-minded, adaptable, and always ready for something new.
